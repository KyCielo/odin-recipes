Cheese Omelette

Description

A filling side dish or as a stand alone, you're sure to love this CHEESY OMELETTE.

2 XL sized eggs and a fist full of shredded cheese is kinda the way to go when it comes to a fulfilling omelette. Any seasonings you want and you are half way there!

Ingredients
  • 2 XL Eggs
  • Seasoning of choice
  • 1 Cup of shredded cheese
  • Non-stick cooking spray

Steps

  1. Pre-heat pan to a medium-high heat.
  2. Apply cooking spray while pan is pre-heating.
  3. Crack eggs into pre-heated pan and scramble gently with a spatula.
  4. Season once eggs settle and cook a little. Pale yellow color.
  5. Place your cheese all around the eggs surface.
  6. Cook until egg is a little runny on the top.
  7. Use your spatula to fold the now flat circular egg in half.
  8. Cook on one side 2mins and season, then flip.
  9. ENJOY!
